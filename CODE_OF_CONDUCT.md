# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to creating a welcoming, respectful and inclusive community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful.
- Be constructive.
- Help other community members.
- Report issues responsibly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment
- Hate speech
- Spam
- Fraud or scams
- Personal attacks
